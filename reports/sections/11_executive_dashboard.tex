    \textbf{Strategic Recommendation Summary:} \\
    Based on the empirical evidence from 2020--2025, a USD 1,000,000 portfolio should overweight AAPL as the core equity holding for risk-adjusted performance. SPY remains the bedrock for broad diversification. BTC should be sized conservatively given its 37\% single-day tail risk, but retained for its unique positive expected return during high-VIX crisis regimes. BDO offers strong inflation protection (43.9\% return during the 2021-2023 inflation spike) and near-zero correlation with US equities, making it a critical diversifier despite its low unconditional return.
