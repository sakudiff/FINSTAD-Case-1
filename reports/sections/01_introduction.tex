Financial markets are influenced by a wide range of economic and market factors that affect asset prices, risk, and investment performance. Investors increasingly rely on data-driven analysis to evaluate investment opportunities across multiple asset classes.
This study aims to build an integrated financial database by combining daily price data for Apple Inc. (AAPL), Banco de Oro Unibank, Inc. (BDO), Bitcoin (BTC), and the SPDR S\&P 500 ETF Trust (SPY) with selected macroeconomic indicators from FRED.
The integrated database enables the application of data acquisition, cleaning, database integration, descriptive statistical analysis, and visualization techniques to examine relationships among financial assets and macroeconomic variables.
The findings of this study are intended to support evidence-based investment decision-making by comparing the performance and risk characteristics of the selected assets.
