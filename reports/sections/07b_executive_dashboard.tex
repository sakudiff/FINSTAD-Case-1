\section{Executive Dashboard}

The interactive executive dashboard provides a consolidated view of the key findings from this study. It displays performance KPIs for each asset, including total return, annualized volatility, Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown, and best/worst trading days. Visualizations include a correlation heatmap, risk-return scatter plot, cumulative returns, drawdown from peak, 30-day rolling volatility, COVID crash analysis, VIX regime comparison, and inflation-hedge performance during high-CPI periods. The dashboard also reproduces the recommended portfolio allocation and descriptive statistics in an accessible format.
