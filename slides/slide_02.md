Limitations
Mon-Fri calendar overcounts tradable days by 10 to 15 days per year per exchange because
exchange-specific holiday lists reduce reproducibility and introduce external dependencies.
LOCF may lag the actual policy rate by up to two weeks during structural breaks such as emergency
FOMC rate cuts because the last known observation remains the best estimate until the next
scheduled release.
Weekend cryptocurrency trading days discarded under the portfolio construction framework
because weekend-only BTC price movements carry no economic weight for a portfolio constrained
to equity market hours.
